Games

In Nova Games, we publish games on itch.io, an Indie Developer program where people can publish their own games and have people play them! The game was released on the 4th of April 2023. Play our game here: https://novagamesofficial.itch.io/beyond-eldorado-forest

About

Nova Games (Made by 2 Year 7/graders) is a company for indie games in Britain. We are a child-friendly place where anyone can play our games and try them out. We are still in our early development stage where we are beginning to start work but not everythings on the list is checked yet. The name of our company (Nova Games) was created by our Head Developer Nemesis. All games and websites were coded by SpicyWater. Our developer who just joined the Nova Team, Joey, is in America. Although he is not active as much as SpicyWater and Nemesis, he gives us some ideas. This is all we can say as of our bio because we have just started. Welcome to Nova.
